About Studio Prangana

Our journey is to create a holistic approach to give our bit to nature while we keep the learning process constant. This is an approach to give life into imagination. We believe architecture and spaces influence the occupant and the nature, hence our idea is to establish a relationship between nature, architecture and its dweller to create influential spaces. This is an effort to create a conscious amalgamation of tradition and technology to create a fine balance. We cater to the needs of clients by understanding their individuality and creating spaces that define them. Client involvement is higly important to us along with our specialised design process as their reviews help us in shaping their dream project. We believe in building up a relationship with clients where we understand their need for particular space and their life style which helps us in providing the best service to make their dreams come true.
